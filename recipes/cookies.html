The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies You'll Ever Have

Description

These are some of the best cookies you'll have in your life. They are gooey, crispy, and oh-so delicious. You'll need a little extra time to make them, but I promise they're worth it.

Ingredients

  • 225g Unsalted Butter (2 Sticks)
  • 280g All-Purpose Flour
  • 3g Baking Soda
  • 4g Kosher Salte
  • 140g White Granulated Sugar
  • 140g Dark Brown Sugar
  • 2 Large Eggs (100g)
  • 10ml Vanilla Extract
  • 110g Semisweet Chocolate Chips
  • 115g 72% Dark Chocolate Bar, rough chopped (Ghiradelli, if you can find it)
  • Maldon Flaky Salt for finishing

Instructions

  1. Whisk together your flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl, and add your your white sugar, eggs, and vanilla to the bowl of a stand mixer. Whisk that mixture until it falls off the whisk in thick ribbons (around 5 minutes)
  2. Melt your butter in a pan or on the microwave until fully liquid. Let that cool to room temp and, using the paddle attachment, mix that into your egg & sugar mixture along with your dark brown sugar.
  3. Now add in your flour mixture and mix on low until just barelu combined. There should be some dry flour left.
  4. Add your chocolate chips and chopped dark chocolate to your mixture and fold in with a spatula.
  5. Place your dough in the fridge, covered with plastic wrap, at least overnight and up to 72 hours.
  6. When ready, preheat your oven to 325F and scoop out your cold cookie dough with a big ice cream scoop onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. You shouldn't be able to fit more than 6-8 cookies per sheet.
  7. Bake until golden brown but still gooey in the middle, around 13-16 minutes, rotating your pans around and back-to-front halfway through
  8. Once removed from the oven, sprinkle with Maldon Flaky Salt and gently press down to get the salt stuck in the top of the cookie, and transfer to a wire rack to cool.
