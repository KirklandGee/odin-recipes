Kirkland's Famous Pizza Recipe

Description

This is the most delicious Neapolitan pizza you'll ever have. Featuring fresh Mozarella, basil, and a delicious tomato sauce, it will change your life.

Ingredients

  • 1000g 00 Flour
  • 670g Filtered Water
  • 20g Kosher Salt
  • 15g High-Quality Olive Oil
  • 20g Honey
  • 15g Active Dry Yeast
  • Fresh Mozarella
  • Fresh Basil
  • Whatever Toppings You Want

Instructions

  1. Heat your water to 95 degrees and add your yeast & honey. Let this sit for 5-10 minutes or until foamy.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer, add your flour, salt, and olive oil, mixing to combine.
  3. While mixing with the dough hook attachment, add in your yeast mixture.
  4. Mix on low speed for 10-15 minutes or until your dough forms a smooth, tight ball. You may need to knead by hand to get it to the texture you want.
  5. Once kneaded, grease the bowl with olive oil, add your dough ball back, and cover with plastic wrap.
  6. Put that bowl in the fridge and let it proof overnight until doubled in size.
  7. Remove your dough from the fridge, punch it down, and separate into 5 or 6 dough balls that are roughly 250g each.
  8. Lay these on a baking sheet and cover with a damp towel to proof for 45 minutes to an hour or until doubled in size. Preheat your pizza oven to at least 750 degrees whil these proof.
  9. Press down one of your dough balls and stretch it into a 12-14 inch round. Place that on a heavily floured pizza peel.
  10. To that round, add your sauce, mozarella, and any other toppings you desire and quickly toss that in your oven.
  11. Rotate your pizza every 20-30 seconds until the cheese is melted and your crust is golden brown with some light charring.
  12. Remove from the oven and top with salt, black pepper, italian seasoning, and a few leaves of fresh Basil.
  13. Repeat with the rest of your dough balls.
