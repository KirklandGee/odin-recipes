Classic Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

These are the kind of pancakes your southern grandma would make. Simple, easy, but also BOMB.

Ingredients

  • 500g Self-Rising Flour
  • 200g Buttermlik
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 20g Maple Syrup
  • 10g Kosher Salt
  • Butter for cooking & syrup for finishing

Instructions

  1. Preheat a griddle or cast iron skillet to medium heat
  2. In a large pyrex measuring cup, whisk your eggs, buttermilk, maple syrup, and salt until combined
  3. Add in your flower, folding slowly with a spatula until just combined and lumpy. The texture should be pretty thick, almost too thick to pour, but not quite.
  4. Throw some butter in your pan until foamy and pour your batter in large rounds, as big as 3-5 inches in diameter. If you have other fillings like blueberries or chocolate chips, sprinkle them in now, once the pancake is on the griddle.
  5. Cook until you see the sides starting to cook and bubbles forming on top, and then flip, cooking both sides until golden brown and cooked through
  6. Top with a little extra butter and maple syrup, and you're good to go!
